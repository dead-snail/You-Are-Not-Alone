enemies:
Stage 1: minotaur                       gain super speed
Stage 2: minotaur, wizard               gain telekenesis
stage 3: minotaur, wizard, phoenix      gain regeneration
stage 4: Ancient Lich                   gain teleportation (mid fight)

minotaur (Josephine): 
    power: has super speed when it charges
    health: 3hp
    dmg: 1 contact dmg
         3 charged dmg
    how to kill: loses 1hp when it charges into a wall
    description: Half human, half bull, the minotaur barrels through walls with great speed. But with speed also comes clumsiness...

wizard: 
    power: shoots ice bolts which slow the player
           can freeze the player temporarily
           has telekenesis to form a shield when in grave peril - prevents 1 attack
    health: 5hp
    dmg: 3 contact dmg
         2 ice bolt dmg
    how to kill: reflect ice bolts using telekenesis to freeze him
    description: An ancient mage placed in the labyrinth to secure you. Freezes you if you get too angy.

phoenix: 
    power: has multiple fire attacks (ring of fire, fireball, etc.)
           regenerates health when it hits you
    health: 5hp
    dmg: 2 contact dmg        |
         5 large fireball dmg |     (0.2dps for 15s)
         1 small fireball dmg |
    how to kill: you must strike it's heart with an iron dagger to send it to ashes
    description: The fiery beast with the magical powers of regeneration turned to the powers of evil by the Ancient Lich.

ancient lich (Murphy): 
    power: cycles through all of the powers of the previous monsters
           regenerates health when she hits you
    health: 15hp
    dmg: 5 contact dmg
         all projectile dmg from other enemies
    how to kill: destroy the lich's phylactery (her crown) without being crushed into the floor yourself
    description: after giving up her mortal body, this mage has stored her soul in her crown - immortalising her for eternity; unless the crown breaks...
