universal powers (for the bosses/enemies) - these have shorter cooldowns than the player:
immense strength - very streng
ice summon - freeze the player
pyrokenesis - fireball
super speed - in the form of charging at the player


legendary powers (for the user) - these all have cooldowns:
super speed - in the form of dashing
reality warping - essentially teleportation
regeneration - returns some health
telekenesis - shield blocks a single attack

not for actual fighting:
flight - cutscenes etc
